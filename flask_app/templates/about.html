About Us

Thanks for visiting RateMyUMDLandlord. This flask app was created by Surmud Jamil, Aashray Raparla, and Eric Qian, for the final project of our python flask development class, CMSC388J, at the University of Maryland. This website allows UMD students to find landlords within the College Park area, rate landlords on a scale from 1-5, and write comments about a specific landlord. With this website, we hope that we can help people looking for housing near the College Park area get a better understanding of their landlords. We understand that it can be frustrating to find out that your landlord isn’t a very nice person, and we hope we can help you make a more informed decision and choose the best place to live.
